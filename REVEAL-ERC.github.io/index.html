Who We Are

Welcome! REVEAL is a joint intitutional team from the University of Graz (Austria), University of St Andrews (United Kingdom), the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (USA) and the Space Telescope Science Institute (USA), focused on uncovering the atmospheres of Earth-like planets outside of our solar system. We are funded by an ERC Synergy Grant. To observe the smallest planets, we must look at the smallest stars. These small stars are quite different than our own Sun. They are highly magnetically active, making it challenging to observe the atmospheres of their planets.
